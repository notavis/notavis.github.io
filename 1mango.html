The
Mango

Mangos are a type of tropical fruit that are native to South Asia, but are now
cultivated in many tropical and subtropical regions around the world. They
have a sweet, juicy flesh that is typically orange or yellow in color and a large,
flat pit in the center. Mangos are a popular fruit that can be eaten raw or used
in a variety of dishes, such as smoothies, salads, and desserts. They are high
in fiber, vitamin C, and vitamin A, and are also a good source of other essential
nutrients. There are many different varieties of mangos with varying sizes,
colours, and flavors. Some popular types of mangos include Alphonso,
Ataulfo, and Tommy Atkins.
