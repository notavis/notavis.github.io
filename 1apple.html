The
Apple

Apples are a type of fruit that are round in shape, typically red,
green, or yellow in colour, and have a firm, juicy flesh. They are a popular
fruit that can be eaten raw or cooked in a variety of dishes, such as pies,
sauces, and cider. Apples are high in fiber and vitamin C, and are also a
good source of antioxidants. They are commonly grown in temperate
regions around the world, and there are many different varieties of
apples with varying flavors and textures. Some popular types of apples
include Granny Smith, Red Delicious, and Honeycrisp.
